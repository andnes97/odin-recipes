Banana Pancakes

Go back to the home page

Ingredients

  • 1 Banana
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 tbs Flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • About 1 dl Milk

Steps

  1. Mush the banana into a bowl.
  2. Add the egg, and whisk it together with the banana.
  3. Add the flour, baking powder and salt to the batter, and mix until it is smooth.
  4. Lastly, add milk until the batter is at the wanted thickness.
  5. Fry the pancakes on medium-high heat until they have a golden brown color.
