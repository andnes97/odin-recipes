Fruit Smoothie

Go back to the home page

Ingredients

  • 1/2 bag Frozen berry/fruit mix
  • Apple juice
  • 1 tbs Natural yoghurt
  • 1 tbs Oats

Steps

  1. Put the berries, fruit, yoghurt and oats into a blender.
  2. Add apple juice until it covers around half of the ingredients.
  3. Blend until it creates a smooth, thick liquid.
  4. Most importantly... enjoy!
