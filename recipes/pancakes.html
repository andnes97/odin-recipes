Norwegian Pancakes (8 pancakes)

Description

Pancakes made like they are in Norway. These pancakes are very thin, and resemble a cr√©pe which you might be more familiar with.

Ingredients

  • 3 dL flour
  • 0.5 ts salt
  • 5 dL milk
  • 4 eggs

Steps

  1. Mix the flour and the salt together in a bowl
  2. Wisk in half the milk until it is a smooth batter
  3. Add the rest of the milk slowly while wisking the batter
  4. Add the eggs
  5. Let the batter rest for 30 minutes
  6. Pour a scoop of the batter into a buttered pan
  7. Lift the pan so the batter is covering the whole pan surface
