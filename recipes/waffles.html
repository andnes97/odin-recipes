Waffles

Description

Easy waffle recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 ts baking powder
  • pinch of salt
  • 4 ts sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbs melted butter

Steps

  1. Preheat waffle iron
  2. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl
  3. Wisk milk and eggs together
  4. Add milk/egg mixture to dry ingredients
  5. Add melted butter
  6. Pour waffle batter over the iron one at a time
