A cheese melt for you hangover

Go back to the home page

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • cheese
  • Butter (Optional)
  • Ketchup / other sauces (Optional)

Steps

  1. Put a slice of bread on a plate.
  2. (Optional) Spread some butter on the slice of bread.
  3. (Optional) Put some ketchup or other sauces on your bread slice.
  4. Put a cheese slice on your bread slice.
  5. Put your masterpiece in the microwave, and heat it for 1 minute on the highest setting.
  6. Enjoy your lazy meal.
